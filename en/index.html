Programs

Smart Government Development Strategy
Improving governmental digitalization and services based on public demands
Service Resilience for the Cloud Generation
Strengthening the resilience of key government networks and services, promoting cloud applications

Monitoring Taiwan’s Digital Development Progress with Digital Well-being
Government Digital Service Guidelines and Website Service Manage
Leading government agencies with building people-oriented digital services
Government Accessible Web Check
Enhancing inclusive, accessible government digital service experiences
My e-Government Portal Share Print
Consolidating government services based on major life events for easy public access .
Common Government Application Services
Cross-agency data transfer, code for Chinese characters, and document format standard
Digital Governance Competency Cultivation
Elevating the government workforce’s digital skills and cross-boundary governance abilities
Government Digital Infrastructure
Operating safe, stable, efficient network and electronic certification environments
Government Open Data
Building a friendly, trusted government open data environment, enhancing data service capacity
Autonomous Use of Personal Data (MyData)
Promoting human-centered data use, allowing the public to download and use personalized data on their own or agree to single-time transfers for third parties to use on-demand, thus creating a more precise digital service
Government Data Standards
Promoting government field data standards, facilitating interdisciplinary data exchange and consolidation
